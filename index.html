About Me

My story

I'm Hasib, a passionate Web Developer based in Bangladesh with a deep love for building clean, functional, and visually compelling digital experiences. My journey into programming began with curiosity — one late night, one tutorial, and suddenly a whole world opened up before me.

I specialize in front-end development, crafting interfaces that are not only pixel-perfect but also performant and accessible. I enjoy working with React, JavaScript, and modern tooling like Vite and Tailwind CSS to bring ideas to life — from concept to deployed product.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me on the football pitch, exploring the latest trends in UI/UX design, or watching tech talks late into the night. I believe great software is built at the intersection of logic and creativity, and I bring both to every project I touch.

I'm currently open to freelance work and meaningful collaborations. If you have a vision, I'd love to help you build it.
